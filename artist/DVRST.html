About the Artist

DVRST
Artist/ Songwriter

Valera Zaytsev, known professionally as "DVRST", exploded onto the Phonk scene with his 2021 viral internet smash "Close Eyes". Born in Russia, his distinguishable production style has quickly amassed him hundreds of millions of streams, placing him among the top dance producers in the world.
